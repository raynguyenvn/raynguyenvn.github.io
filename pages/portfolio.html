About

Attila Vaszka
Web Designer
Bournemouth

Hello!I’m a Multidisciplinary Designer living and enjoying life in sunny Bournemouth, UK. Specialising in Web Design, UI/UX Design, and Visual Development using WordPress and Webflow.

During my career, I have designed more than 100 websites and a number of apps, working closely with both large and small companies to capture the essence of their brand. During my time at edirect in Bournemouth, Web Design projects consisted of collaborating with clients based in the UK, Canada, US, and Dubai. I had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of clients across multiple sectors. 

As a Web Designer, my preference is to work with start-ups whose products or services I feel passionate about.

‍By drawing on this, I can not only help them as a designer, but also help their business to grow. Logo Design, Branding, App Design, 3D Rendering, Video Editing, Motion Graphics, and Product Photography are among the skills I have utilised when working with clients. 

Predominantly, I work as a Web Designer in Bournemouth, but I am currently learning Webflow and I’m working towards mastering how to develop my designs.

‍By working with clients in an honest and transparent manner, we can form a trust that allows us to get the best out of a project. I regard myself as an honest, reliable and up front Web Designer who keeps abreast of new design trends.

hobbies Besides my passion for design, I’m a hobbyist photographer and I have all kinds of gadgets due to my love of learning about new technologies. I also enjoy cycling and travelling with my wife, whom I enjoy exploring new places with.
